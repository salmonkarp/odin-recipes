Coleslaw Recipe

With minimal ingredients and prep work, this top-rated coleslaw dressing is your new summer potluck secret.

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, vinegar, pepper, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and creamy.
  3. Store in an airtight container.
